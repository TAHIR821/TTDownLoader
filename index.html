How To Use TTDownLoader

Copy TikTok URL

Open the TikTok app and find the video you want to download. Tap on the "Share" button and then select "Copy Link" to copy the video URL to your clipboard.

Paste URL

Paste the copied TikTok URL into the input field on our website. Make sure the URL is complete and correctly formatted.

Process Video

Click the "Process" button. Our system will analyze the video and prepare it for download. This usually takes just a few seconds.

Select Format & Quality
